Cloud Storage Setup 

Your IEEE membership includes access to GoogleApps@IEEE, a program focused on providing members with Google Apps Enterprise Edition. This service includes 30gb of cloud-based storage for email and documents stored in your PPCT Library.

 

The PPCT Library also works with other cloud-based storage providers. While some functions will be unavailable, you will be able to store research, generate citations, conduct searches, and build smart collections for your papers. IEEE recommends using Google Drive with PPCT.
